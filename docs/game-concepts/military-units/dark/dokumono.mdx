# Dokumono

## Overview

Dokumono are savage and malevolent creatures that harbor a deep hatred for humans, although they are willing to fight alongside them if well-paid. They are skilled in using poisoned throwing weapons but weak in close combat. Dokumono are classified as Projectile units.
