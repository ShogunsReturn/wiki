# Karasu

## Overview

Karasu are dark creatures with the head and wings of a raven, reincarnations of those who held resentful or arrogant souls in their past lives.  
They soar over the battlefield and descend upon their enemies from the sky, striking fear into opposing armies. Karasu are classified as Cavalry units.
