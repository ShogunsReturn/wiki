Tactician

Overview​

The Tiger Revered Tacticians are masters of warfare, deeply knowledgeable in the art of battle. Regardless of the enemy, they can neutralize their power and turn the tide of a seemingly lost battle.

Key Features​

Cost​

  • Rice: 50
  • Wood: 0
  • Iron: 0
  • Gold: 110
  • Population: 1

Maintenance​

  • Meat: 1.6 /h
  • Gold: 2.0 /h

Training duration​

  • 11 Hours

Honor requirement​

  • 10

Unit type​

  • Rear Units

Trait​

  • Special Effect
Special Effect – Master of War
  • Enhances all units that have a natural bonus against specific enemies, granting an additional damage boost.
  • Functions similarly to Ashihei Captains or Warlords.
  • Applies to units in allied or attached armies, as long as at least one Tactician is present among their ranks.

Tactician Allocation Rule

  • Each Tactician supports 100 troops, with a minimum of 1 Tactician for armies smaller than 100 units.
  • For full effect, 1 Tactician is needed per 100 troops, even if not all troops have bonus effects.
  • Example: 100 Naginata, 100 Bushi, and 200 Ashihei → 4 Tacticians required for full effect.
  • Tacticians do not adjust per turn (e.g., 1 Tactician for Cavalry Charge or 2 for Infantry Combat is incorrect—you need enough for the entire army).

Additional Damage Bonus Calculation

  • Each Tactician adds 0.7 + 0.05 × (level - 1) bonus damage to units with special damage effects.
  • Affects units such as: Bushi vs Ashihei, Lancers vs Cavalry, Masters vs Specials, Fanatics, Karasus, and Cavalry types during charges.

Exceptions:

  • Does not affect Kami or Warrior Monks’ bonus vs Dark Units.
  • Does not affect ranged units.

Bonus Application Example:

  • Ashihei + Ashihei Captains: All Ashihei get +5 damage.
  • Naginata + Tacticians: Bonus applies when fighting Cavalry only.
  • Naginata + Tacticians vs Bushi: No benefit given.

Level Averaging:

  • Different-level Tacticians average their levels (rounded down).
  • Example: 10 Level 1 + 10 Level 3 = 20 Level 2 Tacticians.
