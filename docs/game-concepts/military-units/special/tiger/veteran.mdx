# Veteran

## Overview

<p>
The Tiger Veterans are battle-hardened infantry units known for their extreme discipline.
Accustomed to fighting together, they maximize their effectiveness through mutual support.
These elite troops are deployed to the most critical points of the battlefield.
</p>

---
