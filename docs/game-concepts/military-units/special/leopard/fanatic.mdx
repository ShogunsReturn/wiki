# Fanatic

## Overview

The Leopard Fanatics are warriors driven by the pursuit of an honorable death on the battlefield.
Their sole purpose is to charge headfirst into enemy lines, seeking to inflict as many casualties as possible, fully aware that their fate is glory through sacrifice.

---

### Trait
- Special Effect

<Admonition type="tip" title="Special Effect – Reckless Charge">
  <ul>
    <li>Leopard Fanatics always fight in the front line.</li>
    <li>They charge one turn earlier than other melee infantry, dealing <strong>double damage</strong> (×2 bonus).</li>
    <li>Their aggressive nature comes at a cost—they <strong>take 3× normal damage</strong>.</li>
    <li>They also absorb damage that would otherwise be taken by other infantry units.</li>
  </ul>
</Admonition>
