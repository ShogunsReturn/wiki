# Ashihei Captain

## Overview

The Ronin Ashihei Captains are battle-hardened peasant warriors, promoted for their bravery and dedication on the battlefield.
Their role is to organize and lead the otherwise undisciplined Ashihei troops, maximizing their combat effectiveness.

---

## Key Features

### Cost
- Rice: 50  
- Wood: 20  
- Iron: 10  
- Gold: 150  
- Population: 1

### Maintenance
- Meat: 1.6 /h  
- Gold: 1.8 /h

### Training duration
- 8 Hours

### Honor requirement
- 5

### Unit type
- <Link to="/docs/game-concepts/military-units#melee-infantry">Melee Infantry</Link>

### Trait
- Special Effect

<Admonition type="tip" title="Special Effect – Ashihei Commanders">
  <ul>
    <li>Each Ashihei Captain boosts Ashihei (and Ambusher) units by:</li>
      <ul>
        <li><code>+0.2 + 0.05 × (level - 1)</code> damage per unit</li>
        <li><code>+2 + 0.5 × (level - 1)</code> health per unit</li>
      </ul>
    <li>One captain affects up to 50 Ashihei troops.</li>
    <li>The number of effective captains is capped at <strong>total Ashihei ÷ 50</strong>.</li>
    <li>Maximum bonus:</li>
      <ul>
        <li>+2 damage</li>
        <li>+20 health</li>
      </ul>
    <li>Applies to allied Ashihei units in shared battles.</li>
    <li>If multiple Captains, Warlords, or Tacticians of different levels are present, their level is averaged and rounded down.</li>
  </ul>
</Admonition>

<Admonition type="info" title="Examples">
  <ul>
    <li><strong>490 Ashihei and 10 Captains</strong>: Full bonus of +2 damage and +20 health.</li>
    <li><strong>980 Ashihei and 20 Captains</strong>: Still capped at +2/+20.</li>
    <li><strong>990 Ashihei and 10 Captains</strong>: Bonus is +1 damage and +10 health.</li>
    <li><strong>49 Ashihei and 1 Captain</strong>: +0.2 damage and +2 health.</li>
    <li><strong>48 Ashihei and 2 Captains</strong>: Still +0.2/+2.</li>
    <li><strong>245 Ashihei and 5 Captains</strong>: +1 damage and +10 health.</li>
    <li><strong>240 Ashihei and 10 Captains</strong>: Still +1/+10 (only 5 are effective).</li>
  </ul>
</Admonition>
