# Agonizer

## Overview

The Serpent Agonizers are humanoid creatures with reptilian skin, towering in stature and radiating an aura of terror.
With their deep, monstrous roars, they induce irrational fear, causing enemy troops to flee the battlefield in terror before the fight even begins.
