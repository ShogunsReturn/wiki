# Naginata

## Overview

Naginata units are trained in the use of the naginata, a highly versatile weapon suited for various combat situations. They **excel against cavalry** and are effective in melee combat.

---

## Key Features

### Cost
- Rice: 80
- Wood: 10
- Iron: 20
- Gold: 25
- Population: 1

### Maintenance
- Meat: 1.6 /h
- Gold: 0.9 /h

### Training duration
- 3 Hours

### Honor requirement
- -100

### Unit type
- <Link to="../#melee-infantry">Melee Infantry</Link> specialized in countering cavalry.

### Trait
- <Link to="../#spear-units">Spear Unit</Link>

### Samurai Skills  
They can be affected by the Samurai abilities:  
- <Link to="../../samurais/charisma-skills#exemplary-soldier">Exemplary Soldier</Link>  
- <Link to="../../samurais/charisma-skills#divine-fury">Divine Fury</Link>
