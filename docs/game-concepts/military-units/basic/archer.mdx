# Archer

## Overview

Archers are trained in Kyudo, the art of archery. They rain arrows upon their enemies as they approach, then retreat behind infantry lines to avoid melee combat, where they are highly vulnerable.

---

## Key Features

### Cost
- Rice: 80
- Wood: 50
- Iron: 0
- Gold: 35
- Population: 1

### Maintenance
- Meat: 1.6 /h
- Gold: 0.9 /h

### Training duration
- 4 Hours

### Honor requirement
- -100

### Unit type
- <Link to="../#ranged-infantry">Ranged Infantry</Link> with range of **3**

### Trait
- <Link to="../#ranged-units">Ranged Unit</Link>
